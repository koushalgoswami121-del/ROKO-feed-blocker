Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ROKO background protection.

How does ROKO block feeds without blocking the whole app?

ROKO works beautifully by targeting only the addictive sub-features of the app (like Reels, Shorts, or scrolling video players) as soon as they appear. The normal posts, direct messaging, profile pages, and useful elements of the app remain completely untouched and fully functional, allowing you to stay connected productively!

Is my private scrolling data safe?

Yes, 100%! Your privacy is our highest priority. All filtering, blocking operations, and focus statistics calculations are processed entirely offline and locally on your device. ROKO never monitors, uploads, or shares your search queries, messages, or browsing behavior with any servers.

Does it drain battery or slow down my device?

Not at all! ROKO is highly optimized and lightweight. Its checks execute in microseconds only when one of the monitored apps is launched. During regular use, it sits completely idle. In extensive battery testing, ROKO uses less than 0.8% of battery over a full 24-hour cycle.

Can ROKO help me build permanent focus?

Absolutely! Users report a dramatic improvement in their daily attention spans, focus levels, and time saved within just the first 48 hours of blocking feeds. By breaking the instant-dopamine scrolling cycles, ROKO helps retrain your brain to engage in more productive, long-form habits.

Is pre-registration completely free?

Yes, it is entirely free! By joining our waitlist today, you secure early beta access before the public release, and you will receive exclusive early-bird benefits when ROKO officially launches on the Google Play Store.
