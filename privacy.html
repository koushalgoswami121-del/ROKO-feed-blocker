Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

At ROKO Protection, your privacy is our primary engineering metric. This Privacy Policy explains how ROKO operates under a strict offline-first, zero-knowledge architectural model.

💡 Summary: ROKO does not monitor, collect, upload, or sell your browsing history, message contents, search queries, or personal data. All filtering is done 100% locally and offline on your device.

1. The Accessibility API Service

To detect and block targeted short-form scrolling video feeds (such as Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts), ROKO utilizes the system's Accessibility Service (on Android) or Screen Time APIs (on iOS).

  • Local Monitoring: The Accessibility Service is used solely to identify when targeted feed views enter the active screen.
  • Immediate Cover: When a match is detected, the app renders a local block overlay to block the addictive loop.
  • No Logging: ROKO does not log, save, or store any text input, search terms, passwords, account details, or browsing records parsed by this service.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

ROKO operates entirely in zero-knowledge mode:

  • No Cloud Uplinks: The app does not transmit screen content or interaction data to our servers or any third-party databases.
  • Offline Analytics: Your scrolling block count and saved focus minutes statistics are calculated and stored locally on your device's sandbox partition.
  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not require registration, phone numbers, or account logins to run background protection.

3. Waitlist Sign-Up Data

When you voluntarily sign up for our early-access waitlist on our homepage, the email address you provide is processed solely to notify you when ROKO officially launches on the Google Play Store. You can opt-out or request deletion of your email from the list at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Because ROKO performs all operations locally, we do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, track trackers, or user behavioral SDKs that collect data in the background.

5. Security

All focus settings and blocking lists are isolated within the app's secure private directory. If you enable a lock code under Pro features, that hash is encrypted and saved offline in the device's keystore.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or our offline filtering model, feel free to contact us at support@roko.local.
