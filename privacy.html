Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

At ROKO, protecting your privacy is a core part of how we design our app. Our goal is to help you reduce distracting short-form content while keeping your personal information private.

🛡️ ROKO does not monitor, collect, upload, or sell your personal conversations, browsing history, or keystrokes. Focused on distraction-blocking, built with privacy as a priority.

1. Accessibility Service

ROKO uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect when supported short-form content (such as Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and similar feeds) is displayed on your device.

The Accessibility Service is used only to:

  • Detect supported short-form content on your screen.
  • Display a blocking overlay when enabled.
  • Help you stay focused.

ROKO does not use the Accessibility Service to collect passwords, messages, personal conversations, payment information, or other sensitive information.

2. Data Collection

ROKO is designed to minimize data collection. The app does not collect or transmit:

  • Browsing history
  • Search history
  • Messages
  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Passwords
  • Keystrokes
  • Personal documents

Focus statistics (such as blocked sessions and focus time) are stored locally on your device.

3. Internet Communication

ROKO does not upload screen content or Accessibility Service data to our servers.

If certain optional features require an internet connection (such as update checks or subscription verification), only the minimum information necessary for those services is transmitted.

4. Waitlist

If you voluntarily join our waitlist through our website, we collect the email address you provide solely to notify you when ROKO becomes available.

You may request removal of your email at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

ROKO may use trusted third-party services required for app functionality, such as:

  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions)
  • Google Play Services
  • Crash reporting (if enabled)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

ROKO does not sell your personal information.

6. Security

Your settings, blocking preferences, and focus statistics are stored locally on your device.

Where supported, sensitive local information is protected using Android's security features.

7. Children's Privacy

ROKO is not directed toward children under the age required by applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date.

9. Contact
